Cashew Fruit Juice

Refreshing drink that goes with any meal.

The Cashew Fruit is used by many in Jamaica to make a refreshing drink, that tastes like a cross between a mango and orange. Also known as Cashew Apple the fruit has more vitamin C than an orange. Enjoy our Cashew Fruit Juice recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen Cashews fruit
  • 2 Tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 drop vanilla extract flavoring
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Wash teh chasew fruit and remove the nut
  2. Cut up the fruit into cubes
  3. Put the fruit in a blender with the water and puree
  4. Pour the juice through a strainer into a container. Discard the fiber left in the strainer.
  5. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract flavoring to the container (this step is optional as you can go natural). Mix the juice.
  6. Serve with ice
